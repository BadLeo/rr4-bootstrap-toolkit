# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project follows a professional and respectful collaboration standard. Contributors are expected to communicate clearly, stay constructive in review, and keep discussions focused on improving the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Use respectful, inclusive language
- Assume good intent and discuss disagreements on the merits
- Accept feedback professionally
- Keep issue and pull request discussions relevant and actionable

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, hostile behavior, or repeated bad-faith argument
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any conduct that makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation for conduct that violates these standards.

## Reporting

Report serious conduct issues privately through the security or maintainer contact process documented in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
